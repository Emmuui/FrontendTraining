Tokenomics

  • Seed Investors / Advisors

8.35% of token allocation is distributed to investors and advisors

Seed investors / advisors allotments will be locked for 10 months from the date of the TGE

  • Team

11.67% of token allocation is reserved for core team compensation

Team allotments will be released at a rate of 0.33% per day for the first 10 months from the date of the TGE

  • Pre-Sale

10.85% of tokens were sold at pre-sale.

Pre-sale allotments are released in 25% tranches. Investors will receive 25% at TGE and 25% every month thereafter until they have received their full allocation.

  • IDO

13.85$ of tokens will be offered throughan IDO process, data is TBA

  • Treasury + Bounties

41.67% of token allocation is reserved explicitly for bounties rewarding responders, verifiers and contributors of the Infomatix platform

  • Airdrops, Staking, Liqiudity

13.58% of token allocation will be used primarily for staking rewards and facilitating liquidity with a small portion to be given back to the community in the form of airdrops.

Token Utility

01

Our native BSC token will serve as a unit-of-value token to reward the community of responders and validators on our platform, and allows anyone to participate in the conversion of information to value. This is a novel use case for a token, and unlike any other project the blockchain has been utilized for this far.

02

This structure will enable the project and its founding team to achieve project ambitions to full maturity and gives the project enough flexibility to address requirements that may present post-launch, in readiness for it to become autonomously governed by a DAO.
